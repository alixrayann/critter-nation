About Critter Corner

Critter Corner is a nonprofit organization that has been dedicated to rescuing animals since 2001 and have rescued over 250,000 animals so far. We take in any and all animals brought to us, including dogs, cats, rodents, birds, and reptiles. We take in stray, abandoned, or surrendered animals regardless of their age, health, breed, or temperment. We provide our pets with a safe and loving home to reside in with all the resources they need until they find their new family. In the mean time, we also provide a range of health services, such as vaccinations, microchips, spaying and neutering, and more. We proudly advocate for and care for every animal brought into our care without exception.
